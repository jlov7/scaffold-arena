# Design QA Rubric

## Visual Craft
- Hierarchy clarity
- Spacing rhythm and alignment
- Dark/light consistency
- Motion consistency and restraint

## Interaction Quality
- Predictable control behavior
- Loading and empty states clarity
- Error/retry affordances
- Feedback latency and responsiveness

## Accessibility
- Semantic landmarks
- Dialog semantics and focus trap
- Screen-reader announcements
- Keyboard operability

## Responsive Behavior
- 375px and tablet usability
- No clipped/overlapping controls
- Readable typography at all breakpoints

## Scoring
- 0: Missing / broken
- 1: Partial / inconsistent
- 2: Meets baseline quality
- 3: High quality
- 4: World-class polish
