# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a professional, respectful, and inclusive collaboration environment.

Expected behavior:
- Be constructive and specific in technical feedback.
- Assume good intent while still being rigorous.
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementation, not people.
- Keep discussions respectful across experience levels.

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination or exclusionary language.
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, limit participation, or take other appropriate actions when this code of conduct is violated.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to project maintainers.
